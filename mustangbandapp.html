Mustang Band App

Allows members and followers of the Cal Poly Mustang Band to keep up to date on all aspects of the band!

You can view and e-mail current leadership, check and find information on upcoming events, look up who is in each pep band, listen to music, check the twitter feed, and more!

There is an iOS version of this app in development. It is estimated to be completed in the next few months.

Note: For privacy and protection, all personal information in the screenshots has been blurred. In the application, this data is password protected and is only available to current Mustang Band members.

Calendar

Check upcoming band events on the calendar screen!

Leadership

Look up and contact important members of leadership easily on your phone!

Pep Bands
